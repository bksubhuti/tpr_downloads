Only on this occasion the Exalted One, knowing that in a previous state of existence these monks had devoted themselves to meditation on the Characteristic of Unreality, said to them, “Monks, the Elements of Being are not self-determining, and are therefore unreal.” So saying, he pronounced the following Stanza, {3.407}

 

279. “Unreal are all existing things.”

With wisdom who perceives this fact,

Straightway becomes contemptuous of suffering.

This is the Way of Salvation.
