10.123. 123. Paṭhamasutta - 123. First

13. Parisuddhavagga - 13. Purified

 

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā, nāññatra sugatavinayā.

“Mendicants, these ten things are not purified and cleansed apart from the Holy One’s training.

 

Katame dasa?

What ten?

 

Sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi, sammāñāṇaṃ, sammāvimutti—

Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom.

 

ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.

These ten things are not purified and cleansed apart from the Holy One’s training.”

 

Paṭhamaṃ.
