10.125. 125. Tatiyasutta - 125. Third

13. Parisuddhavagga - 13. Purified

 

“Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā mahapphalā mahānisaṃsā, nāññatra sugatavinayā.

“Mendicants, these ten things are not very fruitful and beneficial apart from the Holy One’s training.

 

Katame dasa?

What ten?

 

Sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāvimutti—

Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom.

 

ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā mahapphalā mahānisaṃsā, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.

These are the ten things that are not very fruitful and beneficial apart from the Holy One’s training.”

 

Tatiyaṃ.
