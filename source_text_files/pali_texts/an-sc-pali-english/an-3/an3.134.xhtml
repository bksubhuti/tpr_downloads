3.134. 134. Parisāsutta - 134. Assemblies

14. Yodhājīvavagga - 14. A Warrior

 

“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, parisā.

“Mendicants, there are these three assemblies.

 

Katamā tisso?

What three?

 

Ukkācitavinītā parisā, paṭipucchāvinītā parisā, yāvatāvinītā parisā—

An assembly educated in fancy talk, an assembly educated in questioning, and an assembly educated to the fullest extent.

 

imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso parisā”ti.

These are the three assemblies.”

 

Dutiyaṃ.
