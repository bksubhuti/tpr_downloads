31. Amatavagga - 31. The Deathless

 

“Amataṃ te, bhikkhave, na paribhuñjanti ye kāyagatāsatiṃ na paribhuñjanti.

“Mendicants, those who don’t enjoy mindfulness of the body don’t enjoy the deathless.

 

Amataṃ te, bhikkhave, paribhuñjanti ye kāyagatāsatiṃ paribhuñjantī”ti.

Those who enjoy mindfulness of the body enjoy the deathless.”
