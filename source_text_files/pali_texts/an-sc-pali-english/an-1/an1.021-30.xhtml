3. Akammaniyavagga - 3. Useless

 

“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ abhāvitaṃ akammaniyaṃ hoti yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, cittaṃ.

“Mendicants, I do not see a single thing that, when it’s not developed like this, is as useless as the mind.

 

Cittaṃ, bhikkhave, abhāvitaṃ akammaniyaṃ hotī”ti.

An undeveloped mind is useless.”

 

Paṭhamaṃ.
