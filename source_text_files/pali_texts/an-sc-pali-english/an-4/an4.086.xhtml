4.086. 86. Oṇatoṇatasutta - 86. Sunk Low

9. Macalavagga - 9. Confirmed

 

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ.

“These four people are found in the world.

 

Katame cattāro?

What four?

 

Oṇatoṇato, oṇatuṇṇato, uṇṇatoṇato, uṇṇatuṇṇato.

~One sunk low who sinks lower, ~one sunk low who rises high, ~one risen high who sinks low, and ~one risen high who rises higher.

 

… Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.

… These are the four people found in the world.”

 

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
