4.120. 120. Dutiyabhayasutta - 120. Perils (2nd)

12. Kesivagga - 12. With Kesi

 

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni.

“Mendicants, there are these four perils.

 

Katamāni cattāri?

What four?

 

Aggibhayaṃ, udakabhayaṃ, rājabhayaṃ, corabhayaṃ—

The perils of fire, water, kings, and bandits.

 

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī”ti.

These are the four perils.”

 

Dasamaṃ.

 

Kesivaggo dutiyo.

 

Kesi javo patodo ca,

 

Nāgo ṭhānena pañcamaṃ;

 

Appamādo ca ārakkho,

 

Saṃvejanīyañca dve bhayāti.
