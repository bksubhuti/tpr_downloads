4.152. 152. Saddhābalasutta - 152. The Power of Faith

16. Indriyavagga - 16. Faculties

 

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni.

“Mendicants, there are these four powers.

 

Katamāni cattāri?

What four?

 

Saddhābalaṃ, vīriyabalaṃ, satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ—

The powers of faith, energy, mindfulness, and immersion.

 

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī”ti.

These are the four powers.”

 

Dutiyaṃ.
