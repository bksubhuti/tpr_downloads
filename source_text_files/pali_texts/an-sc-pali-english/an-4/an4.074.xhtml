4.074. 74. Paṭhamaaggasutta - 74. Best (1st)

8. Apaṇṇakavagga - 8. Guaranteed

 

“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, aggāni.

“Mendicants, these four things are the best.

 

Katamāni cattāri?

What four?

 

Sīlaggaṃ, samādhiggaṃ, paññāggaṃ, vimuttaggaṃ—

The best ethics, immersion, wisdom, and freedom.

 

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri aggānī”ti.

These are the four things that are the best.”

 

Catutthaṃ.
