4.227. 227. Paṭhamavohārapathasutta - 227. Kinds of Expression (1st)

23. Duccaritavagga - 23. Bad Conduct

 

… pe … Adiṭṭhe diṭṭhavādī hoti, asute sutavādī hoti, amute mutavādī hoti, aviññāte viññātavādī hoti … pe … adiṭṭhe adiṭṭhavādī hoti, asute asutavādī hoti, amute amutavādī hoti, aviññāte aviññātavādī hoti … pe ….

“A foolish person … makes much bad karma. … They say they’ve seen, heard, thought, or known something, but they haven’t. … An astute person … makes much merit. … They say they haven’t seen, heard, thought, or known something, and they haven’t. …”

 

Sattamaṃ.
