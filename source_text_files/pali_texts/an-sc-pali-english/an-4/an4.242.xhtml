4.242. 242. Sappurisānisaṃsasutta - 242. Benefits of a Good Person

24. Kammavagga - 24. Deeds

 

“Sappurisaṃ, bhikkhave, nissāya cattāro ānisaṃsā pāṭikaṅkhā.

“Mendicants, you can expect four benefits from relying on a good person.

 

Katame cattāro?

What four?

 

Ariyena sīlena vaḍḍhati, ariyena samādhinā vaḍḍhati, ariyāya paññāya vaḍḍhati, ariyāya vimuttiyā vaḍḍhati—

Growth in noble ethics, immersion, wisdom, and freedom.

 

sappurisaṃ, bhikkhave, nissāya ime cattāro ānisaṃsā pāṭikaṅkhā”ti.

You can expect these four benefits from relying on a good person.”

 

Ekādasamaṃ.

 

Kammavaggo catuttho.

 

Saṅkhitta vitthāra soṇakāyana,

 

Sikkhāpadaṃ ariyamaggo bojjhaṅgaṃ;

 

Sāvajjañceva abyābajjhaṃ,

 

Samaṇo ca sappurisānisaṃsoti.
