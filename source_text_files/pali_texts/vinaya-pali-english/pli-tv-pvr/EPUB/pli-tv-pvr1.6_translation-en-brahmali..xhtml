6. Adhikaraṇavāra

6. The section on legal issues

 

Methunaṁ dhammaṁ paṭisevantassa āpattiyo catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ katamaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ?

When it comes to the offenses for having sexual intercourse, to which of the four kinds of legal issues do they belong?

 

Methunaṁ dhammaṁ paṭisevantassa āpattiyo catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ āpattādhikaraṇaṁ …pe….

They are legal issues arising from an offense. …

 

Anādariyaṁ paṭicca udake uccāraṁ vā passāvaṁ vā kheḷaṁ vā karontassa āpatti catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ katamaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ?

When it comes to the offense for, out of disrespect, defecating, urinating, or spitting in water, to which of the four kinds of legal issues does it belong?

 

Anādariyaṁ paṭicca udake uccāraṁ vā passāvaṁ vā kheḷaṁ vā karontassa āpatti catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ āpattādhikaraṇaṁ.

It is a legal issue arising from an offense.

 

Adhikaraṇavāro niṭṭhito chaṭṭho.

The sixth section on legal issues is finished.
