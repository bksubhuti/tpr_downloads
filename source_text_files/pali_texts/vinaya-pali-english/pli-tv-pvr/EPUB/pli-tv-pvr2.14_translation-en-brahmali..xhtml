6. Adhikaraṇavāra

6. The section on legal issues

 

Kāyasaṁsaggaṁ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ katamaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ?

When it comes to the offenses that are a result of consenting to physical contact, to which of the four kinds of legal issues do they belong?

 

Kāyasaṁsaggaṁ sādiyanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ, āpattādhikaraṇaṁ …pe….

They are legal issues arising from an offense. …

 

Dadhiṁ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ katamaṁ adhikaraṇaṁ?

When it comes to the offenses that are a result of asking for curd and then eating it, to which of the four kinds of legal issues do they belong?

 

Dadhiṁ viññāpetvā bhuñjanapaccayā āpattiyo catunnaṁ adhikaraṇānaṁ—āpattādhikaraṇaṁ.

They are legal issues arising from an offense. …

 

Adhikaraṇavāro niṭṭhito chaṭṭho.

The sixth section on legal issues is finished.
