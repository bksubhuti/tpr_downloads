22.141. 141. Dutiyadukkhasutta - 141. Suffering (2nd)

14. Kukkuḷavagga - 14. Burning Chaff

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Yaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ; tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo … pe …

“Mendicants, you should give up greed for what is suffering. …”

 

yaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ; tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo”ti.

 

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
