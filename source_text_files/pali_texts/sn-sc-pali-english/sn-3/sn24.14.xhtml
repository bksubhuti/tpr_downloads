24.14. 14. Aññaṃjīvaṃaññaṃsarīraṃsutta - 14. The Soul and the Body Are Different Things

1. Sotāpattivagga - 1. Stream-Entry

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṃ upādāya, kiṃ abhinivissa evaṃ diṭṭhi uppajjati:

“Mendicants, when what exists, because of grasping what and insisting on what, does the view arise:

 

‘aññaṃ jīvaṃ aññaṃ sarīran’”ti?

‘The soul and the body are different things’?” …

 

“Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā … pe …

 

niyato sambodhiparāyano”ti.

 

Cuddasamaṃ.
