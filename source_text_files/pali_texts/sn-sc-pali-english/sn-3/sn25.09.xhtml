25.09. 9. Pathavīdhātusutta - 9. Elements

1. Cakkhuvagga - 1. The Eye

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Pathavīdhātu, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

“Mendicants, the earth element is impermanent, perishing, and changing.

 

āpodhātu …

The water element,

 

tejodhātu …

the fire element,

 

vāyodhātu …

the air element,

 

ākāsadhātu …

the space element,

 

viññāṇadhātu aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

and the consciousness element are impermanent, perishing, and changing.

 

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṃ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṃ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī … pe …

Someone who has faith and confidence in these principles is called a follower by faith. …”

 

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

 

Navamaṃ.
