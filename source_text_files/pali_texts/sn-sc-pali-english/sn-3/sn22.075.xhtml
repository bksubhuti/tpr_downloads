22.075. 75. Dutiyasamudayasutta - 75. Origin (2nd)

8. Khajjanīyavagga - 8. Itchy

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Sutavā, bhikkhave, ariyasāvako rūpassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti.

“Mendicants, an educated noble disciple truly understands the origin, the ending, the gratification, the drawback, and the escape when it comes to form,

 

Vedanāya …

feeling,

 

saññāya …

perception,

 

saṅkhārānaṃ …

choices,

 

viññāṇassa samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ pajānātī”ti.

and consciousness.”

 

Tatiyaṃ.
