25.03. 3. Viññāṇasutta - 3. Consciousness

1. Cakkhuvagga - 1. The Eye

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ vipariṇāmi aññathābhāvi;

“Mendicants, eye consciousness is impermanent, perishing, and changing.

 

sotaviññāṇaṃ …

Ear consciousness,

 

ghānaviññāṇaṃ …

nose consciousness,

 

jivhāviññāṇaṃ …

tongue consciousness,

 

kāyaviññāṇaṃ …

body consciousness,

 

manoviññāṇaṃ aniccaṃ vipariṇāmi aññathābhāvi.

and mind consciousness are impermanent, perishing, and changing.

 

Yo bhikkhave … pe …

Someone who has faith and confidence in these principles is called a follower by faith. …”

 

sambodhiparāyano”ti.

 

Tatiyaṃ.
