37.28. 28. Ekasutta - 28. One

3. Balavagga - 3. Powers

 

“Ekena ca kho, bhikkhave, balena samannāgato puriso mātugāmaṃ abhibhuyya vattati.

“Mendicants, when a man has one power he has a female under his mastery.

 

Katamena ekena balena?

What one power?

 

Issariyabalena abhibhūtaṃ mātugāmaṃ neva rūpabalaṃ tāyati, na bhogabalaṃ tāyati, na ñātibalaṃ tāyati, na puttabalaṃ tāyati, na sīlabalaṃ tāyatī”ti.

The power of authority. Mastered by this, a female’s powers of attractiveness, wealth, relatives, children, and ethical behavior do not avail her.”

 

Catutthaṃ.
