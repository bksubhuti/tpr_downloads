35.222. 222. Ajjhattāyatanaaniccasutta - 222. The Interior as Impermanent

17. Saṭṭhipeyyālavagga - 17. Sixty Abbreviated Texts

 

“Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ … pe … jivhā aniccā … pe … mano anicco.

“Mendicants, the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are impermanent.

 

Evaṃ passaṃ … pe …

Seeing this …

 

nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”
