56.064. 64. Surāmerayasutta - 64. Alcohol and Drugs

7. Paṭhamaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga - 7. Abbreviated Texts on Raw Grain

 

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye surāmerayamajjappamādaṭṭhānā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye surāmerayamajjappamādaṭṭhānā apaṭiviratā … pe ….

“… the sentient beings who refrain from alcoholic drinks that cause negligence, are few, while those who don’t refrain are many. …”
