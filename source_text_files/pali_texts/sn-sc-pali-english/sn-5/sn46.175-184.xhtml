Bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttassa punaoghavaggo aṭṭhārasamo.

(To be expanded as in SN 45.171–180.)

 

Ogho yogo upādānaṃ,

 

Ganthā anusayena ca;

 

Kāmaguṇā nīvaraṇā,

 

Khandhā oruddhambhāgiyānīti.

 

(Rāgavinayapariyosāna-dosavinayapariyosāna-mohavinayapariyosānavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

(All should be expanded as in the chapter on removal of greed, hate, and delusion.)

 

(Yadapi maggasaṃyuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ, tadapi bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttaṃ vitthāretabbaṃ.)

(The Linked Discourses on Awakening Factors should be expanded just as the Linked Discourses on the Path.)

 

Bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttaṃ dutiyaṃ.

The Linked Discourses on the Awakening Factors is the second section.
