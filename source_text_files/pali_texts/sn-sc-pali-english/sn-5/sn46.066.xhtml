46.066. 66. Ānāpānasutta - 66. Breathing

7. Ānāpānavagga - 7. Breathing

 

“Ānāpānassati, bhikkhave, bhāvitā … pe …

“Mendicants, when mindfulness of breathing is developed and cultivated it’s very fruitful and beneficial. …”

 

dasamaṃ.

 

Ānāpānavaggo sattamo.

 

Aṭṭhikapuḷavakaṃ vinīlakaṃ,

 

Vicchiddakaṃ uddhumātena pañcamaṃ;

 

Mettā karuṇā muditā,

 

Upekkhā ānāpānena te dasāti.
