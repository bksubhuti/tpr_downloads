56.068. 68. Sāmaññasutta - 68. Respect Ascetics

7. Paṭhamaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga - 7. Abbreviated Texts on Raw Grain

 

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye sāmaññā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye asāmaññā … pe ….
