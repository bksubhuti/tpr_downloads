45.168. 168. Nīghasutta - 168. Troubles

13. Esanāvagga - 13. Searches

 

“Tayome, bhikkhave, nīghā.

“Mendicants, there are these three troubles.

 

Katame tayo?

What three?

 

Rāgo nīgho, doso nīgho, moho nīgho—

Greed, hate, and delusion.

 

ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo nīghā.

These are the three troubles.

 

Imesaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṃ nīghānaṃ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya … pe … ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo”ti.

The noble eightfold path should be developed for the direct knowledge, complete understanding, finishing, and giving up of these three troubles.”

 

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
