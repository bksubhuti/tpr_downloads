16. Kodhapeyyāla - 16. Abbreviated Texts Beginning with Anger

 

“Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā.

“There are these two things.

 

Katame dve?

What two?

 

Kodho ca upanāho ca … makkho ca paḷāso ca … issā ca macchariyañca … māyā ca sāṭheyyañca … ahirikañca anottappañca.

Anger and hostility … offensiveness and contempt … jealousy and stinginess … deceit and deviousness … lack of conscience and prudence.

 

Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā”.

These are the two things.”
