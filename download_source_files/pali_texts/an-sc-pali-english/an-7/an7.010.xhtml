7.010. 10. Macchariyasutta - 10. Stinginess

1. Dhanavagga - 1. Wealth

 

“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, saṃyojanāni.

“Mendicants, there are these seven fetters.

 

Katamāni satta?

What seven?

 

Anunayasaṃyojanaṃ, paṭighasaṃyojanaṃ, diṭṭhisaṃyojanaṃ, vicikicchāsaṃyojanaṃ, mānasaṃyojanaṃ, issāsaṃyojanaṃ, macchariyasaṃyojanaṃ.

The fetters of attraction, repulsion, views, doubt, conceit, jealousy, and stinginess.

 

Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta saṃyojanānī”ti.

These are the seven fetters.”

 

Dasamaṃ.

 

Dhanavaggo paṭhamo.

 

Dve piyāni balaṃ dhanaṃ,

 

Saṅkhittañceva vitthataṃ;

 

Uggaṃ saṃyojanañceva,

 

Pahānaṃ macchariyena cāti.
