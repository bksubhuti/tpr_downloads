5.252. 252. Nissayasutta - 252. Who Should Give Dependence

26. Upasampadāvagga - 26. Ordination

 

“Pañcahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgatena bhikkhunā nissayo dātabbo.

“Mendicants, dependence should be given by a mendicant with five qualities.

 

Katamehi pañcahi?

What five?

 

Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu asekhena sīlakkhandhena samannāgato hoti … pe … asekhena vimuttiñāṇadassanakkhandhena samannāgato hoti.

It’s a mendicant who has the entire spectrum of an adept’s ethics, immersion, wisdom, freedom, and the knowledge and vision of freedom.

 

Imehi … pe … nissayo dātabbo”ti.

Dependence should be given by a mendicant with these five qualities.”

 

Dutiyaṃ.
