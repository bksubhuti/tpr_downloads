4.247. 247. Thūpārahasutta - 247. Worthy of a Monument

25. Āpattibhayavagga - 25. Perils of Offenses

 

“Cattārome, bhikkhave, thūpārahā.

“Mendicants, these four are worthy of a monument.

 

Katame cattāro?

What four?

 

Tathāgato arahaṃ sammāsambuddho thūpāraho, paccekabuddho thūpāraho, tathāgatasāvako thūpāraho, rājā cakkavattī thūpāraho—

A Realized One, a perfected one, a fully awakened Buddha; a Buddha awakened for themselves; a disciple of a Realized One; and a wheel-turning monarch.

 

Ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro thūpārahā”ti.

These four are worthy of a monument.”

 

Pañcamaṃ.
