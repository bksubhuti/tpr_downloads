9. Pamādādivagga - 9. Negligence

 

“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yo evaṃ mahato anatthāya saṃvattati yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, pamādo.

“Mendicants, I do not see a single thing that is so very harmful as negligence.

 

Pamādo, bhikkhave, mahato anatthāya saṃvattatī”ti.

Negligence is very harmful.”

 

Paṭhamaṃ.
