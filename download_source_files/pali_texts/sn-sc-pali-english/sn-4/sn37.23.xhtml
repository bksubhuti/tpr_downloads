37.23. 23. Upaṭṭhitassatisutta - 23. Mindful

2. Dutiyapeyyālavagga - 2. Abbreviated Texts

 

“Upaṭṭhitassati ca hoti, paññavā ca hoti … pe …

“… They’re faithful, conscientious, prudent, mindful, and wise. …”

 

imehi kho, anuruddha, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjatī”ti.

 

Navamaṃ.

 

Ime aṭṭha suttantasaṅkhepā.
