35.225. 225. Bāhirāyatanaaniccasutta - 225. The Exterior as Impermanent

17. Saṭṭhipeyyālavagga - 17. Sixty Abbreviated Texts

 

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, aniccā. Saddā … gandhā … rasā … phoṭṭhabbā … dhammā aniccā.

“Mendicants, sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and thoughts are impermanent.

 

Evaṃ passaṃ … pe …

Seeing this …

 

nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”
