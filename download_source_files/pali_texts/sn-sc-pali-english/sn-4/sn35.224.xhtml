35.224. 224. Ajjhattāyatanaanattasutta - 224. The Interior as Not-Self

17. Saṭṭhipeyyālavagga - 17. Sixty Abbreviated Texts

 

“Cakkhu, bhikkhave, anattā … pe … jivhā anattā … pe … mano anattā.

“Mendicants, the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are not-self.

 

Evaṃ passaṃ … pe …

Seeing this …

 

nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”
