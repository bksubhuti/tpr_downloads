35.227. 227. Bāhirāyatanaanattasutta - 227. The Exterior as Not-Self

17. Saṭṭhipeyyālavagga - 17. Sixty Abbreviated Texts

 

“Rūpā, bhikkhave, anattā. Saddā … gandhā … rasā … phoṭṭhabbā … dhammā anattā.

“Mendicants, sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and thoughts are not-self.

 

Evaṃ passaṃ … pe …

Seeing this …

 

nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.

They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”

 

Saṭṭhipeyyālo samatto.

 

Chandenaṭṭhārasa honti,

 

atītena ca dve nava;

 

Yadaniccāṭṭhārasa vuttā,

 

tayo ajjhattabāhirā;

 

Peyyālo saṭṭhiko vutto,

 

buddhenādiccabandhunāti.

 

Suttantāni saṭṭhi.
