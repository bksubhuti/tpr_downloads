25.06. 6. Rūpasaññāsutta - 6. Perception

1. Cakkhuvagga - 1. The Eye

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Rūpasaññā, bhikkhave, aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī;

“Mendicants, perception of sights is impermanent, perishing, and changing.

 

saddasaññā …

Perception of sounds,

 

gandhasaññā …

perception of smells,

 

rasasaññā …

perception of tastes,

 

phoṭṭhabbasaññā …

perception of touches,

 

dhammasaññā aniccā vipariṇāmī aññathābhāvī.

and perception of thoughts are impermanent, perishing, and changing.

 

Yo, bhikkhave, ime dhamme evaṃ saddahati adhimuccati, ayaṃ vuccati ‘saddhānusārī … pe …

Someone who has faith and confidence in these principles is called a follower by faith. …”

 

sambodhiparāyano’”ti.

 

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
