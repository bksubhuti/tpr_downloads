22.142. 142. Tatiyadukkhasutta - 142. Suffering (3rd)

14. Kukkuḷavagga - 14. Burning Chaff

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Yaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo … pe …

“Mendicants, you should give up desire and greed for what is suffering. …”

 

yaṃ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ; tatra vo chandarāgo pahātabbo”ti.

 

Sattamaṃ.
