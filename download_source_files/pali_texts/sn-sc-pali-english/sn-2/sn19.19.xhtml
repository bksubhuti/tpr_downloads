19.19. 19. Pāpasikkhamānasutta - 19. A Bad Trainee Nun

2. Dutiyavagga - 2. The Second Chapter

 

“Addasaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vehāsaṃ gacchantiṃ.

“I saw a trainee nun flying through the air.

 

Tassā saṅghāṭipi ādittā … pe …

Her outer robe was burning …” …

 

pāpasikkhamānā ahosi … pe ….

“She used to be a bad trainee nun …”

 

Navamaṃ.
