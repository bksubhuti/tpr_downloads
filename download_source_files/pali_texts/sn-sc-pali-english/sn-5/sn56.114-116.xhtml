56.114-116. 114–116. Nirayamanussanirayādisutta - 114–116. Dying in Hell and Reborn as Humans

11. Pañcagatipeyyālavagga - 11. Abbreviated Texts on Five Destinations

 

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye nirayā cutā manussesu paccājāyanti; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye nirayā cutā niraye paccājāyanti … pe … tiracchānayoniyā paccājāyanti … pe … pettivisaye paccājāyanti … pe ….

“… the sentient beings who die in hell and are reborn as humans are few, while those who die in hell and are reborn in hell, or the animal realm, or the ghost realm are many.”

 

Pannarasamaṃ.
