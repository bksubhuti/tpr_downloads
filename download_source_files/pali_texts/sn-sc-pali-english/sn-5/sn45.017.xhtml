45.017. 17. Dutiyaparisuddhasutta - 17. Purified (2nd)

2. Vihāravagga - 2. Meditation

 

Sāvatthinidānaṃ.

At Sāvatthī.

 

“Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā anaṅgaṇā vigatūpakkilesā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā.

“Mendicants, these eight things don’t arise to be purified, bright, flawless, and rid of corruptions apart from the Holy One’s training.

 

Katame aṭṭha?

What eight?

 

Seyyathidaṃ—sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāsamādhi.

They are: right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, and right immersion.

 

Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā anaṅgaṇā vigatūpakkilesā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā”ti.

These eight things don’t arise to be purified, bright, flawless, and rid of corruptions apart from the Holy One’s training.”

 

Sattamaṃ.
