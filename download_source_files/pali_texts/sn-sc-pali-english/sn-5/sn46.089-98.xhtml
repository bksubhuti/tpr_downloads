46.089-98. 89–98. Tathāgatādisutta - 89–98. A Realized One, Etc.

10. Appamādavagga - 10. Diligence

 

“Yāvatā, bhikkhave, sattā apadā vā dvipadā vā catuppadā vā bahuppadā vā”ti vitthāretabbaṃ.

“Mendicants, the Realized One, the perfected one, the fully awakened Buddha, is said to be the best of all sentient beings—be they footless, with two feet, four feet, or many feet …”

 

Appamādavaggo dasamo.

 

Tathāgataṃ padaṃ kūṭaṃ,

 

mūlaṃ sārena vassikaṃ;

 

Rājā candimasūriyā ca,

 

vatthena dasamaṃ padanti.

 

(Appamādavaggo bojjhaṅgasaṃyuttassa bojjhaṅgavasena vitthāretabbo.)

(To be expanded as in SN 45.139–148.)
