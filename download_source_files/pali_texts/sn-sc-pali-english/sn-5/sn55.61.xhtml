55.61. 61. Paññāvepullasutta - 61. The Increase of Wisdom

6. Sappaññavagga - 6. A Wise Person

 

“…. Paññāvepullāya … saṃvattantī”ti.

“Mendicants, when four things are developed and cultivated they lead to the increase of wisdom. …”

 

Ekādasamaṃ.

 

Sappaññavaggo chaṭṭho.

 

Sagāthakaṃ vassaṃvutthaṃ,

 

dhammadinnañca gilānaṃ;

 

Caturo phalā paṭilābho,

 

vuddhi vepullatāya cāti.
