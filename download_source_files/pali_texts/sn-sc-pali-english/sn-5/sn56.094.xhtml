56.094. 94. Tulākūṭasutta - 94. False Weights

10. Catutthaāmakadhaññapeyyālavagga - 10. Abbreviated Texts on Raw Grain

 

… “Evameva kho, bhikkhave, appakā te sattā ye tulākūṭakaṃsakūṭamānakūṭā paṭiviratā; atha kho eteva bahutarā sattā ye tulākūṭakaṃsakūṭamānakūṭā appaṭiviratā … pe ….

“… the sentient beings who refrain from falsifying weights, metals, or measures are few, while those who don’t refrain are many. …”
